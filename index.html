Our Story

Made with
intention

"I started Lumo in my kitchen because I wanted candles that actually felt like home — not just a scent, but a whole atmosphere."

Every Lumo candle begins with a simple belief: your space should feel like a refuge. We source our botanicals from small farms, hand-pour in small batches, and obsess over every detail — from the weight of the wick to the curve of the vessel.

— Priya, Founder of Lumo

Why Lumo

Crafted to last.
Made to matter.

All-Natural Ingredients

We use only sustainably sourced soy wax, cotton wicks, and phthalate-free fragrance oils — safe for you, your family, and the planet.

Handpoured in India

Each candle is handcrafted in our Mumbai studio, ensuring consistent quality, intentional design, and the kind of care that machines simply can't replicate.

50+ Hour Burn Guarantee

We stand behind every candle we make. If your burn time falls short of 50 hours, we'll replace it — no questions, no hassle, no exceptions.
